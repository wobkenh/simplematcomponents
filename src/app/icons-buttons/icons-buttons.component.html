Icons and Buttons

You can display all material style button types (normal, raised, icon). You can specify an on click on all cells, even if they have no button. With no button type specified, the cell will turn into a clickable cell. You can add an icon derived from the cells data to a column. You can also combine buttons and icons as you wish.
