Footer row

Use the withFooter method on any TableColumn and a footer row will appear. Further customize the styling of your footer row using footerRowNgStyle and footerRowNgClass on simplemattable and customize the styling of single footer cells using withFooterNgStyle and withFooterNgClass on a table column.
